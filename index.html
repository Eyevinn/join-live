Join Live Broadcast

📺 How it works:

  1. Start Camera - Allow camera access to preview your video
  2. Join Live - Connect to the broadcast (you'll be visible to the producer)
  3. Wait for Selection - The producer will select when to put you on air
  4. Watch for Indicators - You'll see notifications when going live

🔴 On-Air Indicators:

  • 5...4...3... Countdown: You're going on air in seconds!
  • ● ON AIR Live Now: You're currently broadcasting to viewers

💡 Tip: Stay ready once you join - the producer can put you on air at any time!
